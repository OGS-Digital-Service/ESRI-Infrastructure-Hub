Federal Guidance Updates

  • Bipartisan Infrastructure Law (BIL)department of energy (doe)
    Department of Energy Releases Proposed Interpretive Guidance on Foreign Entity of Concern for Public Comment

    On December 1st, the U.S. Department of Energy released a notice of its proposed guidance and a request for public comment on its proposed interpretation of the statutory definition of “foreign entity of concern” (FEOC) in the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law, which is designed to limit the participation of FEOCs within government-supported domestic battery supply chains and bolster the growth of domestic and friend-shored battery materials processing and manufacturing.

  • Inflation Reduction Act (IRA)Internal Revenue Service (IRS)
    Treasury, IRS provide guidance for those who manufacture new clean vehicles

    On December 1st, Department of the Treasury and the Internal Revenue Service issued two items of guidance on the excluded entity restriction of the section 30D clean vehicle credit, as amended by the Inflation Reduction Act.
